# Andrej Karpathy

Andrej Karpathy is a AI researcher who described the LLM Wiki pattern.

## About

Former director of AI at Tesla, former senior director of research at OpenAI. Known for his work on computer vision, language models, and AI education.

## Contributions in this Wiki

Original author of the LLM Wiki idea that this wiki is based on.
