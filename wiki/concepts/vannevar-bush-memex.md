# Vannevar Bush's Memex

**Memex** is the vision of a personal knowledge machine described by Vannevar Bush in his 1945 essay "As We May Think."

## Core Vision

A Memex is:
- A personal, curated knowledge store
- Supports associative trails (links) between documents
- The connections between documents are as valuable as the documents themselves
- Private, actively curated by the user

## Relationship to LLM Wiki

Karpathy notes that LLM Wiki is closer to Bush's original Memex vision than the modern web. The key insight from Memex that LLM Wiki carries forward:

- Associative linking between related pieces of knowledge
- Personal curation
- The value is in the connections

What Bush couldn't solve: **who would do the maintenance**. LLM Wiki solves this - the LLM does all the maintenance.
