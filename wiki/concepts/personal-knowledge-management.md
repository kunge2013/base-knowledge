# Personal Knowledge Management

Personal Knowledge Management (PKM) is the practice of collecting, organizing, and retrieving personal knowledge over time.

## LLM Wiki Approach

LLM Wiki brings automation to PKM:
- User curates sources and asks questions
- LLM does all the tedious bookkeeping: summarizing, cross-referencing, updating, maintaining
- Knowledge stays current because the LLM handles maintenance

## Use Cases

- Tracking personal goals, health, psychology
- In-depth research on a topic over weeks or months
- Reading books - building out companion wikis with characters, themes, connections
- Competitive analysis, course notes, hobby deep dives
