# Knowledge Accumulation

**Knowledge Accumulation** is the principle that knowledge should build incrementally over time, rather than being recreated from scratch for every query.

## In LLM Wiki

In the LLM Wiki pattern, every ingest and every valuable query result leaves the wiki richer than before:
- New sources integrate into existing pages
- New connections are discovered and cross-referenced
- Contradictions are flagged when new information conflicts with old claims
- Valuable queries are saved as new pages for future reference

## Contrast with Stateless Approaches

Stateless approaches like standard RAG don't accumulate knowledge. Every query starts from the raw chunks. Knowledge never compounds.
