# Machine Learning Research

LLM Wiki is well-suited for in-depth machine learning research over extended periods of time.

## How LLM Wiki Helps

- Read papers/articles one at a time, incrementally build out the wiki
- Evolving thesis develops gradually as more sources are ingested
- Concepts get refined as new information comes in
- Contradictions between papers are flagged
- Cross-references naturally build out the connections between ideas
